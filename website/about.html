The Plan...
To solve a problem within the 'transport' scope that would aid people in navigating around Melbourne's public transport network beyond the reach of other solutions.

We first diversified our ideas of what could be provided and then narrowed in on the aspects that were most useful to commuters in a web application medium.

The Details...
Road to Nowhere tackles the problem with a subscription based notification system. Users select their favourite lines, routes, stations or destinations and 'subscribe' to updates concerning them. The updates come from a community driven reporting system, alike to a social network. It also provides other features including an interactive delay map.
